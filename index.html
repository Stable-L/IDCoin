About ID Coin

ID Coin (IDC) is a cryptocurrency asset designed as a stable digital currency with an exchange rate of 1 Rupiah pegged to 1 United States Dollar (USD) on the Tron network.

IDC is pegged to a 1 USD equivalent exchange mechanism, providing consistent value stability for users, businesses, and the digital financial ecosystem.

Built on the efficient blockchain network TRON (TRC20), ID Coin combines transaction speed, low fees, and on-chain transparency.

  • Everyday digital transactions
  • Store of value
  • Cross-platform payments
  • DeFi and Web3 integration

With fixed supply and secure contract design, IDC ensures trust and transparency.

Note: ID Coin is a community-driven crypto asset and not affiliated with any government or central bank.
